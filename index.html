TSFMO

Details

We introduce TSFMO, a benchmark for Tracking Small and Fast Moving Objects. This benchmark aims to spur research in developing more accurate and innovative methods for this particually challenging task. TSFMO consists of 250 sequences with about 50k annotated frames, all of which have been manually annotated with a bounding box. To the best of our knowledge, TSFMO is the first benchmark dedicated to tracking small and fast moving-objects, particular in sports applications.

The complete TSFMO dataset(~4.03G) can be downloaded here: "https://share.weiyun.com/ScF3FdzL"

All results of our method on TSFMO can be downloaded here: "https://share.weiyun.com/mqyL1WjJ"
